Restaurant

Experience Culinary Delights at Cortijo de Tajar Hotel Restaurant

The Cortijo de Tajar Hotel Restaurant has a dining room with a 2-seater category. It is a magnificent refectory open to the general public, and is made up of two separate rooms, decorated in different traditional styles characteristic of western Granada.

This particularity serves to create two environments where you can choose the most comfortable place to enjoy the most typical products and gastronomy that our land offers, and which are prepared daily by our head chef Jorge Pacheco and his great team of kitchen professionals.

The gastronomic offer of our restaurant is committed to the fusion of the most avant-garde signature cuisine with the most traditional recipe books of Andalusia.

Indulge in Culinary Excellence at Our Restaurant

Among our gastronomic specialties we can highlight dishes typical of the healthy, varied and nutritious Mediterranean diet, the culinary knowledge of Andalusia, and the rich Arab legacy of Al Andalus.

Likewise, dishes cooked with purple green asparagus, a typical product of Huétor Tájar recognized with a Specific Designation of Quality, are of special relevance for the most exquisite palate. In our cafeteria menu you will also find artisanal portions and dishes, hams, cheese, sausages, pickles and a long etcetera that will delight the most demanding palates.

Our restaurant has the distinctive “RURAL QUALITY” brand of Poniente Granada, which was awarded in 2008.

Experience Gourmet Excellence

It is an artisanal appetizer in which you will have the opportunity to taste an exquisite typical tapa made with top quality products native to Granada and surrounding areas in a gastronomic bet that fuses signature cuisine and traditional cuisine.

The renowned restaurant team at the Cortijo de Tájar Hotel Restaurant, with its chef Jorge Pacheco at the helm, offers you the possibility of organizing any type of event wherever you want, with a total guarantee of service and, always offering the highest quality standards, which our prestigious cuisine is known for.

If you are interested in organizing a meal in our restaurant, we have both a la carte and buffet service, or a wide portfolio of menus. Request detailed information about our services without any type of commitment. We are sure we can give you what you need.

Hotel

Discover the Charm of Cortijo de Tájar Rural Hotel

Our rural hotel stands in the middle of a natural setting rich in lush orchards that still preserve the sweet flavor of Al Andalus, and its way of doing things, respecting the environment, an invaluable source of the productive wealth of the low plain of the west. Granada.

The Cortijo de Tajár Rural Hotel has been designed and created to offer a welcoming environment to tourists where they can savor the well-achieved fusion between the rustic tradition characteristic of this last frontier of Al Andalus, with the comfort that the modernity of a European country offers us.

The Hotel Cortijo de Tájar has a room with capacity for 100 people where you can organize your events, work meetings, business meals, presentations of products and services, team building days, etc. in a relaxed, welcoming and professional environment.

Embrace Tranquility and Tradition with us

We have tried to combine the quality and comfort of our era, with the warm and welcoming aromas inherent to country homes in a Granada from other times, times of farmhouses and farm gardens. We have tried to take you to a bucolic setting where what was truly important were the people and their physical and mental balance.

In our rural hotel, you can find a fantastic restaurant with a 2-course category where you will undoubtedly enjoy exquisite dishes that contain the most traditional flavors combined with the exotic touches of the new signature cuisine, washed down with wines of a, more than well, chosen winery, all with the help of a respectful and very professional service. A luxury for the senses that does not unbalance your wallet.

The Experience of Enjoying in Every Detail

The rooms at the Cortijo de Tájar hotel are all created and provided with the quality and comfort elements typical of a modern hotel loaded with the charm of simple, peaceful, pleasant things that the lover of rural tourism tirelessly seeks.

From our rooms, generous windows open onto the lower plain of Granada and its exuberant countryside dyed with the multiple colors that this rich nature generously reveals in a harmonious symphony of flowers, fruits, vegetables and greens.

We offer you a series of general and in-room services that will make you forget the vertigo and the obligations that daily life imposes on you, so that your visit to our beautiful province of Granada is so rewarding that you want to return.
