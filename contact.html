The Cortijo de Tájar Rural Hotel is located in the heart of western Granada, just 30 minutes from the emblematic city of Granada, 50 minutes from cosmopolitan Málaga and its Costa del Sol, 1 hour and 20 minutes from the world city olive oil, Jaén; 1 hour and 50 minutes from the emblematic city of the caliphs, Córdoba; just 2 hours from the adventurous Almería, 2 hours and 20 minutes from the monumental city of Seville…

Located in a rural environment, the Hotel Rural Cortijo de Tájar has easy access by car from the A-92 Highway, exit 203 Huétor Tájar (Granada).

Do you Want to Work with Us

Be part of our family

Imagine working in an environment where the Genil River whispers stories through the poplar trees, and where fertile fields and orchards overflow with fresh produce that will inspire every dish you serve. At Cortijo de Tajar, we not only invite you to a job; We invite you to an experience in which gastronomy is intertwined with the richness of the rural environment.

If you are excited by the idea of working in an environment where the passion for cooking meets the beauty of the rural environment, we want to meet you. Send your resume and a cover letter to the contact email, telling us why you would love to be part of Cortijo de Tajar.

At Cortijo de Tajar, your professional growth is a priority. We offer training and development opportunities so you can hone your skills and advance your culinary career.

In Cortijo de Tajar, you will not only find a job; You will find a home where your creativity, dedication and love of gastronomy will be celebrated. Join us on this exciting culinary adventure and be part of a story that is woven with each dish we serve.
