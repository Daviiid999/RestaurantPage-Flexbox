Get to Know Us a Little Lore

Welcome to Cortijo de Tajar

On the banks of the Genil River, surrounded by the fertile orchards and extensive groves that adorn the low plains of Granada, the Hotel Restaurant Cortijo de Tajar looks with devotion to the Sierra Nevada.

A place where you can savor all the charm of the typical Andalusian farmhouses, the cozy and cheerful white villages that color the south of Spain, nature in its purest form and achieve the well-being that you do not find in the course of your daily life, a life full of noise and rush that stress your body and mind.

The Cortijo de Tájar Rural Hotel has been created to free you from all that negative charge and satisfy your needs with all the comforts you may need in a rural environment of extraordinary charm and beauty.

Our History

In the heart of the enchanting Granada countryside, nestled along the serene banks of the Genil River, the story of Hotel Restaurant Cortijo de Tajar unfolds like a lyrical tale woven with the threads of tradition, nature, and tranquility.

Generations ago, the founders of the Cortijo de Tajar envisioned a haven where weary souls could escape the hustle and bustle of modern life, finding solace amidst the beauty of Andalusian landscapes.

As the first stones were laid, the Cortijo de Tajar began to take shape, rising as a testament to the founders' devotion to preserving the essence of the region. The architecture mirrors the warmth and hospitality of Andalusia, with every nook and cranny echoing the authenticity of rural life.

Enter our Kitchen

The Cortijo de Tajar Hotel Restaurant has a dining room with a 2-seater category. It is a magnificent refectory open to the general public, and is made up of two separate rooms, decorated in different traditional styles characteristic of western Granada.

This particularity serves to create two environments where you can choose the most comfortable place to enjoy the most typical products and gastronomy that our land offers, and which are prepared daily by our head chef Jorge Pacheco and his great team of kitchen professionals.

The gastronomic offer of our restaurant is committed to the fusion of the most avant-garde signature cuisine with the most traditional recipe books of Andalusia.
